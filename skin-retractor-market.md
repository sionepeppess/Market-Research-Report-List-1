<p><p>The skin retractor market is segmented into various types based on the number of prongs or "caws." Two caw retractors are designed for simple procedures, providing easy access with minimal tissue retraction. Three caw retractors offer enhanced stability and are suitable for moderate-depth surgeries. Four caw retractors are best for complex surgeries, ensuring optimal exposure of deeper tissue layers. Each type serves specific surgical needs, emphasizing the importance of choosing the right tool for effective and safe procedures.</p></p>
