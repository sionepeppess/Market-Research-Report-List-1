<p><strong>The Chemical Studs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Injectable Adhensive Anchors</li><li>Capsule Adhensive Anchors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chemical studs market consists of two primary types: injectable adhesive anchors and capsule adhesive anchors. Injectable adhesive anchors are applied directly into drilled holes, allowing for greater customization and flexibility in construction and repair applications. In contrast, capsule adhesive anchors come pre-packaged within capsules that dissolve upon mixing with a hardener, simplifying the installation process. Both types enhance the strength and durability of connections in various industries, including construction, automotive, and manufacturing, by providing reliable bonding solutions.</p></p>
