<p><p>Building and construction tapes for roofing are available in various materials, each serving specific functions. Polypropylene tapes are lightweight and flexible. Polyethylene tapes provide moisture resistance. Foil tapes excel in heat reflection, and paper tapes are often used for masking and temporary applications. Foam tapes offer cushioning and sealing properties, addressing diverse roofing needs across the market.</p></p>
