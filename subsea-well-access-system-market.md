<p><p>The Subsea Well Access System market comprises two primary types: vessel-based and rig-based well access systems. In contrast, rig-based well access systems are integrated with drilling rigs, providing a more stable platform for well intervention and maintenance activities. Both systems facilitate efficient exploration and production in challenging underwater environments, catering to the growing offshore energy demands.</p></p>
