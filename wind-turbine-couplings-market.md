<p><p>The wind turbine couplings market includes various types designed to connect the turbine components effectively. Composite-disc couplings feature lightweight, flexible designs that absorb shocks and accommodate misalignments, enhancing operational efficiency. Normal driveline couplings, typically made from robust materials, transmit torque and rotational motion between turbine parts, ensuring stability and reliability under high loads. Both types play crucial roles in optimizing energy transmission and prolonging the lifespan of wind turbine systems. Their selection depends on the specific operational requirements and environmental conditions.</p></p>
