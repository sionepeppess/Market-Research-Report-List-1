<p><p>The AC clamp meters market is divided into two main types: AC voltage and AC current. AC voltage clamp meters measure the electrical potential difference in circuits, allowing users to monitor voltage levels without interrupting the circuit. Meanwhile, AC current clamp meters measure the flow of alternating current through conductors, enabling efficient diagnosis of electrical systems. Both types are essential tools for electricians and technicians, providing non-invasive measurements that enhance safety and accuracy in electrical diagnostics and maintenance.</p></p>
