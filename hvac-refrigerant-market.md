<p><p>The HVAC refrigerant market is primarily categorized into three types: fluorocarbons, hydrocarbons, and inorganic compounds. Fluorocarbons, such as HFCs, are widely used due to their efficient thermal properties but have environmental concerns due to greenhouse gas emissions. Hydrocarbons, like propane and isobutane, are natural refrigerants that possess low global warming potential but require stringent safety measures. Inorganic refrigerants, including ammonia and water, are often used in industrial applications for their efficiency and environmentally friendly profile but may have handling challenges.</p></p>
