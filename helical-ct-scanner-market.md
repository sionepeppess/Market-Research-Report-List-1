<p><p>The helical CT scanner market is categorized into double layer and multi-layer helical CT scanners. Double layer helical CT scanners utilize two detectors to capture images simultaneously, enhancing image quality and reducing scan times. In contrast, multi-layer helical CT scanners incorporate multiple detector layers, allowing for even faster imaging and improved resolution. These advancements support a wide range of applications in medical diagnostics, offering benefits like reduced radiation exposure and improved visualization of anatomical structures, thereby catering to diverse clinical needs.</p></p>
