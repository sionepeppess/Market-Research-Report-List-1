<p><p>The disposable syringe market comprises mainly two types: conventional syringes and safety syringes. Conventional syringes are standard medical devices used for injecting fluids or withdrawing samples, commonly made from plastic and designed for single use. Safety syringes incorporate features that minimize the risk of needle-stick injuries, such as retractable needles or shielding mechanisms, promoting safer handling and disposal. Both types are crucial for enhancing patient safety, reducing infection risks, and improving overall healthcare efficacy during medical procedures.</p></p>
