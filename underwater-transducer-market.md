<p><strong>The Underwater Transducer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Frequency</li><li>Intermediate Frequency</li><li>High Frequency</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The underwater transducer market is categorized into three types based on frequency. Low frequency transducers, typically below 1 kHz, are used for long-range applications like underwater communication and habitat mapping. Intermediate frequency transducers, ranging from 1 kHz to 10 kHz, balance range and resolution, making them suitable for sonar systems and marine monitoring. High frequency transducers, above 10 kHz, offer precise imaging and detailed data for applications such as fish finding and underwater structural inspections. Each type serves distinct purposes in underwater environments.</p></p>
