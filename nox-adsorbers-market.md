<p><strong>The NOx Adsorbers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active NOx Adsorbers</li><li>Passive NOx Adsorbers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The NOx adsorber market is divided into two main types: active NOx adsorbers and passive NOx adsorbers. Active NOx adsorbers utilize a regeneration process that involves injecting fuels or oxidants to convert stored NOx into harmless nitrogen, thus enhancing efficiency and performance. In contrast, passive NOx adsorbers rely on natural conditions for the adsorption and release of NOx, making them simpler and more cost-effective, but potentially less efficient under varying operating conditions. Both types serve to reduce nitrogen oxide emissions in various applications.</p></p>
