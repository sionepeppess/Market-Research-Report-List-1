<p><p>Construction liability insurance encompasses various market types, including Directors and Officers (D&O) Insurance and Errors and Omissions (E&O) Insurance. D&O Insurance protects corporate leaders from personal losses due to lawsuits related to their company decisions. E&O Insurance, on the other hand, safeguards professionals from claims of inadequate work or negligence while providing services. Both types are essential in the construction sector, addressing the unique risks and responsibilities faced by companies and professionals in managing projects and client expectations.</p></p>
