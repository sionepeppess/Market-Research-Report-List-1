<p><ul><li>Pulse Dose</li><li>Continuous Flow</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical oxygen generators market can be categorized into two main types: pulse dose and continuous flow systems. Pulse dose oxygen generators deliver oxygen in controlled bursts, optimizing usage during inhalation, making them efficient for patients with varying oxygen needs. Continuous flow generators, on the other hand, provide a steady stream of oxygen, ensuring a constant supply regardless of the patient's breathing pattern. Each type serves different clinical requirements, enhancing patient comfort and treatment effectiveness in various healthcare settings.</p></p>
