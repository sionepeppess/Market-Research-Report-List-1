<p><p>The Electrostatic Oil Cleaner (EOC) market is categorized into two main types: Single Phase and Three Phase Electrostatic Oil Cleaners. Single Phase EOCs utilize single-phase power supply to effectively remove contaminants from oil, making them suitable for smaller applications or facilities with limited power availability. In contrast, Three Phase EOCs leverage a three-phase power supply, allowing for higher efficiency and capacity, ideal for larger industrial setups that require extensive oil purification and processing capabilities. Both types enhance oil longevity and performance.</p></p>
