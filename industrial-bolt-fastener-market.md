<p><ul><li>Half Screw Bolt</li><li>Full Screw Bolt</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial bolt market consists of various types of fasteners, notably half screw bolts and full screw bolts. Half screw bolts feature partially threaded shafts, allowing for quick assembly and disassembly while providing strong clamping forces. They are commonly used in applications requiring frequent adjustments. In contrast, full screw bolts have a fully threaded design, offering enhanced tension distribution and gripping strength, making them ideal for high-load applications. Both types serve critical roles in ensuring the structural integrity of industrial assemblies.</p></p>
