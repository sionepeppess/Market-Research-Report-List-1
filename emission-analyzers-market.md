<p><ul><li>Non-dispersive Infrared (NDIR) Analyzers</li><li>Flame Ionization Detector (FID)</li><li>Chemiluminescence Analyzer (CLA)</li><li>Constant Volume Sampler (CVS)</li><li>Other Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The emission analyzers market is characterized by distinct types: Non-dispersive Infrared (NDIR) analyzers measure gas concentration using infrared light absorption. Flame Ionization Detectors (FID) identify and quantify organic compounds by ionizing them in a flame. Chemiluminescence Analyzers (CLA) detect nitrogen oxides through light emitted from chemical reactions. Constant Volume Samplers (CVS) collect emissions at a consistent volume for analysis. Other types include various specialized devices suited for specific pollutants or applications, broadening the market's versatility.</p></p>
