<p><p>The elastic strain sensor market is segmented primarily into resistive, capacitive, and other types. Resistive strain sensors operate on the principle of resistance change due to deformation, making them widely used for precise strain measurements. Capacitive strain sensors, on the other hand, detect changes in capacitance caused by strain, offering high sensitivity and less noise interference. Other types include optical and piezoelectric sensors, each with unique applications, advantages, and limitations, catering to diverse industrial and research demands.</p></p>
