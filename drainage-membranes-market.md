<p><p>The drainage membranes market includes several types, each suited for specific applications. HDPE drainage membranes are known for their high-density polyethylene composition, offering excellent durability and chemical resistance. Rubber drainage membranes provide flexibility and are ideal for waterproofing. PP drainage membranes, made from polypropylene, are lightweight and resistant to environmental stress. ABS drainage membranes, crafted from acrylonitrile-butadiene-styrene, offer impact resistance. Non-woven drainage membranes, composed of fibrous materials, provide effective filtration and drainage solutions in various construction projects.</p></p>
