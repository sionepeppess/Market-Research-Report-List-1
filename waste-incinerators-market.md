<p><p>The waste incinerators market is segmented into several types, including rotary kiln, fluidized bed, static hearth, and others. Static hearth incinerators burn waste on a stationary base, offering simplicity but lower efficiency. Other types may include modular and specialized systems, catering to diverse waste materials and processing needs in waste management.</p></p>
