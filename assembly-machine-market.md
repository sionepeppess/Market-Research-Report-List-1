<p><p>The assembly machine market comprises fully automatic and semi-automatic assembly machines. Fully automatic assembly machines operate independently, efficiently handling high-volume production with minimal human intervention, ideal for large-scale manufacturing. In contrast, semi-automatic assembly machines require some manual input, providing flexibility for varying production scales and tasks. These machines typically balance automation and human oversight, making them suitable for medium-scale operations or tasks needing customization. Both types enhance productivity and accuracy in manufacturing processes across various industries.</p></p>
